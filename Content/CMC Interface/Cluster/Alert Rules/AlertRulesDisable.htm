Disable an Alert Rule

[CMC Interface]

To temporarily disable an alert rule, in the CMC's Alert Rules page select the checkbox to the left of the rule and then click Disable on the lower right of the page. If you want you can disable multiple rules at the same time by selecting mutiple rules' checkboxes and then clicking Disable.

A disabled rule will remain in your Rules list display, but the rule will no longer be applied by the HyperStore system. In the rules list a disabled rule is distinguished by its name being greyed out.

To re-enable a rule that has been disabled, select the checkbox to the left of the rule and then click Enable on the lower right of the page. The HyperStore system will resume applying the alert rule.
