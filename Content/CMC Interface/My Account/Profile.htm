Profile

[CMC Interface]

Path: Drop-down menu under your user name (at top right) → Profile

Supported tasks:

  • Change your contact information
  • View your canonical user ID

Change Your Contact Information

  1. Update your contact information.
  2. Click Save.

Changing your system administrator email address in this page does not impact the sending of system notification emails. To change the email address to which notification emails are sent, use the Default Email Address to Receive Notifications setting on the Configuration Settings page.

View Your Canonical User ID

The information displayed in the Account Profile page includes your "Canonical ID". The system automatically generates a unique canonical ID for every user. It is not editable.
