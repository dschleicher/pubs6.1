Delete a Rating Plan

[CMC Interface]

In the CMC's Rating Plans page click Delete to the right of the name of the plan that you want to delete. After you confirm that you want to take this action, the rating plan will be deleted from the system.

If you delete a plan that is currently assigned to some users, those users will be automatically switched to their group default rating plan, or to the system default rating plan ("Default-RP") if no group default plan has been set.

You cannot delete the default rating plan, or the "Whitelist-RP" plan (which supports the HyperStore whitelist feature).
