Rating Plan

[CMC Interface]

Path: Users & Groups → Rating Plan

Supported tasks:

  • Add a Rating Plan
  • Edit a Rating Plan (CMC)
  • Delete a Rating Plan (CMC)

The Rating Plan page is for creating and maintaining rating plans. It is not for assigning a rating plan to users. To assign a rating plan to a group of users, use the CMC's Manage Groups interface. To assign a rating plan to a specific user, use the Manage Users interface.

For an overview of HyperStore user billing functionality, see Billing Feature Overview.
