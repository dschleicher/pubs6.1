Licensing and Auditing

A valid Cloudian software license is required to run HyperStore software. Evaluation licenses are available as well as production licenses. Before using HyperStore software, you must obtain a license from support@cloudian.com or from your Cloudian sales representative.

License Expiration

When you reach the warning period preceding your license expiration, then in the Cloudian Management Console (CMC), the top of the console displays a warning that the expiration date is approaching.

When you reach your license expiration date you enter a grace period, per the terms of your contract. During the grace period:

  • In the CMC, the top of the console screen displays a warning indicating that your license has expired.
  • Every S3 response returned by the S3 Server includes an expiry HTTP response header (name: x-gemini-license; value: Expired: <expiry_time>)

When you reach the end of your grace period after the license expiration date:

  • No service is available for end users. All incoming S3 requests receive a "503 Service Unavailable" error response. The response also includes the expiry header described above.
  • You can still log into the CMC to perform system administration functions (including applying an updated license), but you will not be able to access users' stored S3 objects.

License Updating

You may need to update your license periodically, depending on the specific terms of your Cloudian license agreement. Updating your license requires obtaining a new license file from Cloudian and applying that file on all of your HyperStore nodes.

You can use the CMC to dynamically apply a new license file to your HyperStore system. In the CMC, go to the Cluster Information page to install a new license file.
